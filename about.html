Our Journey

Built on
trust and quality

Riyan Foods and Spices LLP was established with a singular vision: to bring the finest Indian agri-commodities — spices, grains, pulses, millets, and dehydrated products — to global markets with complete transparency and consistency.

From our base in Bhavnagar, Gujarat, we have built direct relationships with farmers and processing partners across Rajasthan, Andhra Pradesh, Tamil Nadu, Gujarat, and Madhya Pradesh, ensuring farm-to-port traceability at every step.

Fully certified from inception — APEDA, FSSAI, Spices Board, IEC, and GST registered — we serve importers, food manufacturers, and distributors across 48+ countries in Africa, Southeast Asia, the Middle East, Europe, and North America. Every shipment carries our commitment to quality, documentation accuracy, and on-time delivery.
