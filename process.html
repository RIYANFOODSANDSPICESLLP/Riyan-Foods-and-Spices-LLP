How We Work

From farm to your
warehouse — step by step

Transparency at every stage. Here's exactly how we source, process, test, pack, and ship your spice order — no surprises.

The Full Process

8 steps, built for reliability

01
🌿
Sourcing & Procurement
Sourcing through verified farmers, traders, and mandis across Rajasthan, Andhra Pradesh, Gujarat, and Madhya Pradesh, with traceability maintained at supplier and batch level.
  • Supplier network across key producing regions
  • Mandi procurement at major markets
  • Seasonal sourcing aligned with availability
  • Storage arranged as per product requirements
02
🔎
Incoming Quality Check
Incoming lots are inspected and sampled based on quality parameters such as moisture, foreign matter, and overall condition.
  • Visual grading and initial quality check
  • Moisture and basic parameter evaluation
  • Selection based on required specifications
  • Supplier evaluation based on consistency
03
⚙️
Cleaning & Processing
Products undergo cleaning and processing as required to achieve desired quality and specifications.
  • Cleaning through appropriate machinery
  • Sorting and grading based on requirements
  • Steam sterilisation where applicable
  • Processing aligned with product specifications
04
🔬
Quality Evaluation
Quality checks are conducted based on buyer requirements and product specifications prior to packaging.
  • Parameters evaluated based on product type
  • Moisture and physical characteristics checked
  • Organoleptic evaluation (aroma, colour, appearance)
  • Samples retained as required
05
🏛️
Third-Party Testing
Accredited third-party laboratory testing is arranged as required, based on destination market and buyer specifications.
  • Pesticide, aflatoxin, and microbiological testing as required
  • Testing scope based on destination requirements
  • Reports provided as applicable
  • Compliance aligned with buyer specifications
06
📦
Packaging
Products are packed as per buyer requirements, including standard bulk and private label packaging options.
  • PP woven, HDPE, vacuum, or other formats
  • Standard 25 kg / 50 kg or custom sizes
  • Private label and branding options
  • Custom packaging subject to MOQ
07
🚢
Shipping & Logistics
Shipments are planned and executed based on order requirements, with FCL and LCL options via major Indian ports.
  • FCL and LCL shipment options
  • Ports: Mundra, JNPT, Chennai
  • Fumigation arranged as required
  • Flexible logistics planning
08
📋
Documentation
Export documentation is arranged based on shipment and destination requirements.
  • COO, CoA, phytosanitary, fumigation as applicable
  • Bill of Lading and shipping documents
  • Support for LC and compliance documentation
  • Documentation aligned with buyer needs
