Testing Protocol

Every batch is
tested before dispatch

Quality checks are supported through in-house processes and, where required, accredited third-party laboratory testing prior to shipment.

01
Incoming Material Inspection
Visual inspection, moisture check, purity grading, and foreign matter assessment at sourcing stage.
02
In-House Lab Testing
Colour value (ASTA), volatile oil content, curcumin %, moisture, and organoleptic properties tested in-house.
03
Third-Party Lab Verification
Pesticide residue (EU MRL), aflatoxin (B1+B2+G1+G2), heavy metals, and microbiological tested by accredited lab.
04
Certificate of Analysis (CoA)
Certificate of Analysis (CoA), phytosanitary certificate, and fumigation reports are provided as required, based on shipment and destination requirements.
