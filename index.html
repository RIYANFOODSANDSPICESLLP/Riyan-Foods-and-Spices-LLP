How We Work

Our export process, simplified

01
🌿
Farm Sourcing
Direct procurement from verified farmers and manufacturers across Rajasthan, Andhra Pradesh, Gujarat, and Madhya Pradesh, with full traceability.
02
🔬
Quality Testing
In-house and third-party lab testing conducted as required, covering parameters such as moisture, pesticide residue, aflatoxin, ASTA colour value, and microbiology.
03

Steam sterilisation, grading, and custom packaging with private label printing on PP woven, HDPE, or vacuum bags. Custom packaging options are subject to minimum order quantities (MOQ).

FCL/LCL shipments through Mundra, JNPT, and Chennai, with documentation support provided as per shipment requirements, including COO, CoA, phytosanitary, fumigation, and LC processes.
