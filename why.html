What makes us different

Every importer asks the same question: why you? Here are the key factors clients consider when working with Riyan.

01
Certified & Compliant
Registered with APEDA, FSSAI, Spices Board, IEC, and GST. Products are handled in line with applicable export and destination requirements.
  • Testing conducted as per buyer or market requirements
  • Aflatoxin and pesticide checks available if required
  • CoA and related documents provided as applicable
  • Phytosanitary certificate arranged when required
02
Sourcing Network
Sourcing through verified farmers, traders, and mandis across Rajasthan, Andhra Pradesh, Gujarat, Tamil Nadu, and Madhya Pradesh.
  • Traceability maintained as per batch and supplier
  • Flexible sourcing based on availability
  • Focus on consistent specifications
  • Efficient supply chain coordination
03
Reliable Supply
Supply planning supported by storage and sourcing network to ensure smooth order fulfillment.
  • Dispatch timelines aligned with orders
  • Proper storage practices followed
  • Advance communication on seasonal changes
  • Flexible shipment planning
04
Transparent Pricing
Clear and competitive pricing based on product specifications, packaging, and shipment terms.
  • FOB / CIF / CFR options available
  • Pricing based on order volume
  • No hidden charges
  • Market updates on request

Private label and bulk packaging options available in PP woven, HDPE, and vacuum formats.

Export Support
Direct communication and support provided throughout the export process, from inquiry to shipment.
